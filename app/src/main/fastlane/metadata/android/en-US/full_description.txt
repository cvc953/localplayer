Local Player is a native Android audio player designed for simplicity, elegance, and an immersive listening experience.

Built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3, it offers a modern interface with a strong focus on advanced lyrics support.

Key Features:
• Synchronized Lyrics: Full support for .lrc (line-by-line) and .ttml (precise word-by-word) lyric files.
• Media3 Engine: Powered by the latest Media3/ExoPlayer for high-fidelity and stable playback.
• Modern UI: Minimalist Material Design 3 with dynamic theme support.
• Visual Adaptation: Interface colors adapt to the current album art using androidx.palette.
• Privacy First: No ads, no trackers, and no internet required. Your music stays private.
• Formats: Supports MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, and more.

Local Player is an open-source project (MIT License) focused on providing a functional, fast, and user-respecting media experience.
